      Membership

      Membership is not open yet. The association is currently being established and its registration is in progress. Membership will open once registration is complete and the first General Assembly has adopted the fee schedule. The details below are the planned structure. To be notified when membership opens, write to info@collectivedynamics.org.

      Membership will be open to anyone who supports the aims of the association — researchers, practitioners, and students across all regions and disciplines. International participation is intended by design.

      Who can join

      Any individual who supports the association’s aims (Statutes §4). Membership is for individuals — there is no corporate or institutional membership.

      Membership types

      • Voting members — the default. Full rights: take part in the General Assembly, vote, stand for election, and receive all member benefits.
      • Students & early-career researchers — a reduced rate (see fees). They are voting members with the same rights.
      • Non-voting members — admitted by the Executive Board for advisory or supporting roles, without taking part in formal governance.

      How it will work

      Once membership opens, there will be two ways to join:

      1. At a conference — tick the membership option when you register for PED or TGF.
      2. Any time — a short online sign-up, or by writing to the Executive Board.

      For now, if you’d like to join when membership opens, register your interest by writing to info@collectivedynamics.org with your name and affiliation, and we’ll get in touch.

      Questions?

      See the FAQ for details on voting rights, the Steering Committee, and how the association is run — or email info@collectivedynamics.org.
