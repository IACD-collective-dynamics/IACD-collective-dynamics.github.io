## Executive Board (Vorstand, §6)

The Executive Board (§26 BGB) runs the day-to-day business of the association and
represents it jointly (two board members together).

| Role | Name |
|------|------|
| Chair | Andreas Schadschneider |
| Deputy Chair | Jun Zhang |
| Treasurer | Mohcine Chraibi |

*Elected at the founding meeting for a two-year term.*

## Financial Auditors (Kassenprüfer, §12)

Two financial auditors, who are not members of the Executive Board, review the
accounts and the annual financial report and report to the General Assembly.

## Steering Committee (§11)

The Steering Committee is the scientific advisory body providing strategic guidance
for the PED and TGF conferences. Its members are elected or confirmed by the
General Assembly.
