## Mission

The IACD gives a lasting organisational and legal home to the international
community studying collective dynamics — pedestrian and evacuation dynamics, crowd
science, traffic and granular flow, and related fields.

Our field has always been built across borders and disciplines: physicists,
engineers, mathematicians, computer scientists, and psychologists working on the
same questions. The IACD exists to keep that community open and together — across
countries, disciplines, and career stages — and to safeguard the continuity and
quality of the conferences it grew from.
