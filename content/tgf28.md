## About the series

Traffic and Granular Flow brings together researchers studying the dynamics of
interacting many-particle systems — vehicular and pedestrian traffic, granular
materials, and related collective phenomena — across physics, engineering,
mathematics, and the social sciences. TGF28 is organised under the umbrella of the
IACD, which provides the conference's long-term organisational continuity.

More details — programme, registration, and key dates — will follow. To stay
informed, watch [collective-dynamics.eu](https://collective-dynamics.eu/index.php/cod/tgf)
or write to [info@collectivedynamics.org](mailto:info@collectivedynamics.org).
