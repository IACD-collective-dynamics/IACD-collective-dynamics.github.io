Membership is open to anyone who supports the aims of the association —
researchers, practitioners, and students across all regions and disciplines.
International participation is intended by design.

## Who can join

Any individual who supports the association's aims (Statutes §4). Membership is for
**individuals** — there is no corporate or institutional membership.

## Membership types

- **Voting members** — the default. Full rights: take part in the General Assembly,
  vote, stand for election, and receive all member benefits.
- **Students & early-career researchers** — a reduced rate (see fees). They are
  **voting members** with the same rights.
- **Non-voting members** — admitted by the Executive Board for advisory or
  supporting roles, without taking part in formal governance.

## Fees

*Proposed structure, pending approval by the General Assembly.*

| Who | Fee |
|-----|-----|
| Regular | **€50 / year** — or **€90 / 2 years** |
| Students & early-career | **Free** if an existing member nominates you, otherwise **€10 / year** |
| Anyone attending PED or TGF | **Free** for that membership term |

- Your membership runs **from the day you join** (12 or 24 months), not the
  calendar year.
- If you register for a **PED or TGF conference**, membership is **free** — just
  tick "become a member" on the registration form.

## How to join

1. **At a conference** — tick the membership option when you register for PED or TGF.
2. **Any time** — write to **[info@collectivedynamics.org](mailto:info@collectivedynamics.org)**
   with your name and affiliation, and the Executive Board will process your
   admission.

A short online sign-up form will follow once the membership system is live.
